Jingwei is often regarded as a symbol of futile resistance.

How could a small bird possibly fill an endless sea with stones and twigs?

But the anger of a small bird matters too.

Sometimes, individual power appears insignificant in the face of the violence of absolute power. Yet even when one is drowned, hidden, or buried by the sea, throwing a stone, throwing a twig, may already be one of the most important acts of our time.

Most of my work is ultimately based on one simple principles:

Do not forget too easily.
